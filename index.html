Biography

I am a PhD student in Communications Systems Division, Electrical Engineering Dept, Linkoping University (LiU), Sweden. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Erik G. Larsson and Prof. Emil Bjornson. I am working on signal processing algorithms for cell-free massive MIMO networks which is envisaged to be a potential technology beyond 5G.

You can find more about me here: My CV and blog.
